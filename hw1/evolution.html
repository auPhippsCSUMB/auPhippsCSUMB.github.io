A (Brief) History of HTML


HTML was developed in the late 1900s by Tim Berners-Lee as a solution to formatting webpages. HTML was develoepd using a markup language format, which is typically used for quick formatting of documents. When a tag (brackets for html, could be #'s or something else other places) wraps of predicates a piece of text, the text gets formatted accordingly. HTML became the official web standard in 2000, and since its creation, there have been many implementations.
